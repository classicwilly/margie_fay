# WORK TETRAHEDRON

**Career resilience mesh. Boss as bottleneck is dead.**

---

## The Problem: Work Hub-and-Spoke Collapse

### Current System = Single Point of Failure

**Boss as hub:**
- Boss toxic ‚Üí your job becomes hell
- Boss leaves ‚Üí your projects die
- Boss plays favorites ‚Üí you're invisible
- Boss doesn't advocate ‚Üí you're passed over for promotion
- Boss gets laid off ‚Üí you're next

**Result: CASCADE COLLAPSE**

You can't do your job ‚Üí stress increases ‚Üí performance suffers ‚Üí you get fired or quit ‚Üí income stops ‚Üí family crisis ‚Üí Memorial Fund activates.

**The bottleneck kills careers.**

---

## Hub-and-Spoke Work Failures

### 1. Boss Overwhelmed
**1 manager, 12 direct reports.**
- Cannot give individualized career development
- Misses your burnout signals
- No time for mentorship
- Only sees you during crisis (performance review)
- Promotion decisions made without context

### 2. Peer Isolation
**Everyone competing, no one collaborating.**
- Can't ask for help (will look weak)
- Don't share knowledge (hoarding = job security)
- No one to cover when you're sick
- Toxic comparison culture
- Layoffs pit you against colleagues

### 3. No Income Stability
**One job loss = financial catastrophe.**
- All eggs in one basket (one employer)
- Severance only lasts 2-3 months
- Unemployment doesn't cover bills
- Savings depleted in 60 days
- Shame prevents asking for help

### 4. Burnout Invisible Until Breakdown
**No one sees it coming.**
- Working 60-hour weeks (normalized)
- Sleep deteriorating (pushed through)
- Anxiety increasing (white-knuckled)
- Physical symptoms (ignored)
- Then: Breakdown, medical leave, or quit without plan

### 5. Career Transitions Terrifying
**Pivot alone in the dark.**
- No idea what skills transfer
- No network in new industry
- Imposter syndrome paralysis
- Can't afford to fail (bills don't stop)
- Give up, stay in soul-crushing job

**The hub fails ‚Üí career dies ‚Üí income stops ‚Üí you believe you're unemployable.**

---

## The Solution: Work Mesh

### Complete K‚ÇÑ Graph for Career Resilience

**4 Vertices (Work Tetrahedron):**
1. **You** (blue) - Own your career data, track skills/burnout/goals
2. **Manager/Boss** (green) - Provides resources, removes blockers, advocates
3. **Mentor/Advisor** (purple) - Career guidance, industry perspective, long-term strategy
4. **Peer/Colleague** (amber) - Collaboration, skill exchange, mutual support

**6 Edges (Support Flows):**
1. **You ‚Üî Manager**: Daily work coordination, resource requests, performance feedback
2. **You ‚Üî Mentor**: Career strategy, skill development, industry navigation
3. **You ‚Üî Peer**: Knowledge sharing, collaboration, emotional support
4. **Manager ‚Üî Mentor**: Your career development alignment, advocacy coordination
5. **Manager ‚Üî Peer**: Team collaboration, project coordination, skill visibility
6. **Mentor ‚Üî Peer**: Industry connections, skill validation, opportunity awareness

**NO SINGLE POINT OF FAILURE.**

When boss toxic ‚Üí Mentor and Peer see it, help you navigate or exit safely.  
When burnout approaching ‚Üí All 3 adults see early signs, coordinate intervention.  
When layoff coming ‚Üí Manager/Mentor/Peer mobilize network, you land softly.

**The mesh catches you.**
