# Advisory Board & Interview Plan

## Purpose
Create a short advisory board and interview plan to ensure co-design for neurodivergent users. The advisory board will provide continuous feedback and validate early product decisions.

## Scope
 
 
 Advisory board: 4–6 community advisors (lived experience) + 2 clinical advisors (optional) + 1 privacy/ethics advisor.
 
 
 Interviews: 8–12 formative interviews over 2 weeks with diverse neurodivergent participants.

 
 
 `wonky_advisory_interactions_total` — incremented each time an advisory session occurs (logged to Prometheus/telemetry).
